About us

Software for the people who show up.

pplCRM is one list for constituents, voters, donors and volunteers, built for the offices, campaigns and non-profits that hold communities together.

Why we built it

The gap between a spreadsheet and an enterprise contract.

Walk into a constituency office, a local campaign headquarters or a small non-profit and you will usually find the same thing: a heroic spreadsheet. Casework in one tab, donors in another, last year’s volunteers in a file somebody’s predecessor emailed around. It works until it doesn’t, and it always stops working in the busiest week of the year.

The alternatives were not built for this work. Sales CRMs want to move deals to "closed", but a constituent is never closed. Enterprise political tech is priced for national organizations and takes a consultant to set up. Email platforms charge for every contact you store and quietly own your list on the way through.

So we built the tool we kept wishing existed: one shared list where the person who knocked a door, gave twenty dollars and emailed about a pothole is one person, not three rows in three systems. Simple enough that a volunteer is productive their first morning. Priced so a two-person office can afford it. And honest about the one thing that matters most: the list is yours, not ours.

pplCRM is built in Canada by a small independent team. Your data lives in Canada, everything exports whenever you want, and delete means deleted. We would rather keep those promises than grow fast by breaking them.
