Campaigns are separate working contexts that share one contact list. Your office is the permanent context; election campaigns are created before a race and archived after it. Contacts, households, and tags are shared across all of them — what stays separate per campaign is supporter data (support level, voting status), email consent, newsletters, forms, donations, canvassing, and the inbox.

Only admins and owners manage campaigns or switch between them. Editors and Viewers belong to exactly one campaign and everything they see and do stays in it. Assign a member to a campaign from the Users page (or when inviting them); unassigned members work in the office context. Archiving a campaign moves its members back to the office.
